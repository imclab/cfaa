What is the CFAA?

The Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) is so broad that law enforcement says it criminalizes all sorts of mundane Internet use.

You could be committing a federal crime.

How can the CFAA affect me?

Potentially even breaking a website’s fine print terms of service agreement. Don’t set up a Myspace page for your cat. Don’t fudge your height on a dating site. Don’t share your Facebook password with anybody.

This is bad for everyone.
